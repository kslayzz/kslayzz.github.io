Who We Are

A One-Stop Solution for Every Financial Need

KMA is a Pune-based Chartered Accountancy firm offering a complete suite of consultancy and compliance services. We eliminate the need for multiple service providers by combining traditional financial expertise with modern automation ensuring accuracy, honesty, and deep domain knowledge at every step.

Our young, result-oriented team takes ownership of outcomes, strives to exceed expectations, and brings diverse industry experience across sectors and geographies serving clients from India to North America, Europe, and the UAE.
