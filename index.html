Audit & Assurance
Credible financial information is the bedrock of sound business decisions. Our audit practice goes beyond tick-box compliance — we examine internal controls, assess risk exposures, and provide management with clear, actionable insights. Whether a statutory audit, internal review, or tax audit, every engagement is led by a partner who understands your business context and is accountable for the outcome.

02
Direct Taxation
Navigating India's direct tax landscape demands both technical depth and commercial awareness. We advise on income tax planning, return filing, DTAA structuring, FEMA compliance, and expatriate taxation — ensuring you are always in a position of strength. When disputes arise, our litigation team represents clients effectively before the tax authorities and appellate forums, including under the Black Money Act.
DTAAFEMAExpatriatesLitigationBlack Money Act
03
Transfer Pricing
Cross-border related-party transactions are among the most scrutinised areas of international tax. Our transfer pricing practice provides end-to-end coverage — from transaction structuring and economic benchmarking to full documentation, annual filings, and Advance Pricing Agreement negotiations. We also advise on BEPS compliance and CBCR obligations across our 7+ country network, helping clients achieve certainty and avoid costly adjustments.
APABEPSBenchmarkingDocumentationAnnual Compliance
04
Indirect Tax & GST
GST compliance, done right, is more than just filing returns on time. Our team handles the complete monthly and quarterly compliance cycle — GSTR-1, GSTR-3B, GSTR-2B reconciliation — and proactively identifies mismatches before they become notices. We advise on classification, input tax credit optimisation, reverse charge implications, and represent clients in GST audits, assessments, and appellate proceedings with a track record of successful outcomes.
GST ReturnsITC ReconciliationAnnual AuditAdvisoryLitigation
05
Bookkeeping & MIS
Clean books are the foundation of every good business decision. Our outsourced bookkeeping service covers end-to-end transaction recording, TDS and GST health checks, bank reconciliation, and accounts closure. We layer on top a structured MIS — monthly P&L, balance sheets, cash flow statements, and performance dashboards — giving leadership a real-time financial picture. Leveraging automation tools, we keep your accounts audit-ready at all times with minimal disruption to your team.
P&L ReportingDashboardsAutomationReconciliationAudit-Ready Accounts
06
Virtual CFO
Not every business needs a full-time CFO, but every business benefits from one. Our Virtual CFO service gives startups and growing companies access to strategic financial leadership — without the overhead. We take ownership of financial planning, budgeting, investor reporting, fund-raise readiness, banking relationships, and working capital management. As your business evolves, your Virtual CFO scales with it, acting as a genuine financial partner at the decision table.
Financial StrategyInvestor ReportingBudgetingFund-Raise PrepWorking Capital
07
Corporate & Secretarial
Corporate compliance is not merely a legal obligation — it is the framework within which business trust is built. We handle company incorporation, Registrar of Companies filings, board and general meeting management, and ongoing ROC compliance. For transactions and restructurings, we conduct comprehensive due diligence, advise on governance frameworks, and provide end-to-end support for mergers, acquisitions, demergers, and cross-border structuring exercises.
ROC / MCAM&ADue DiligenceGovernanceRestructuring
08
Startup & SME Advisory
Early-stage and growing businesses face a unique set of financial challenges — from choosing the right entity structure and managing early cash flows to preparing for investor scrutiny and scaling operations. KMA has deep experience working with founders and entrepreneurs, providing pragmatic, plain-spoken advice that translates into real outcomes. We act as a long-term financial partner across the full growth lifecycle — from pre-incorporation through to exit readiness.
Entity StructuringFounder AdvisoryESOP PlanningValuationsExit Readiness
09
International Tax
For businesses operating across borders, tax planning must anticipate multiple regulatory regimes simultaneously. We advise on inbound and outbound structuring, permanent establishment risks, withholding tax obligations, and treaty eligibility — covering India's treaty network and the geographies we serve directly. Whether you are an Indian company expanding abroad or a foreign entity entering India, our team brings the cross-border fluency to structure your affairs correctly from the outset.
Inbound StructuringPE RiskWHT AdvisoryTreaty PlanningPOEM Analysis
